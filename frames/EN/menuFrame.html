You are a gunner on the allied space station protecting the planet. Our reconnaissance probes indicate that 5 hostile pigs have entered orbit around our base planet. Your mission is to destroy them as quickly as possible.
Can you get them before they get you?

If you are ready pressed on GO.

How to Play
You have 3 types of ammunition:-
Bullets:
slow and the least powerful, but you have a lot and they replenish quickly.
Laser:
very fast and powerful, but replenishes more slowly.
Missile:
fast and the most powerful, but replenishes very slowly.
Bullets are set by default. To select a different one click/touch on the selector panel at the top left of the screen.
You can control your gun with any combination of mouse, keyboard or touch. To review or set keyboard keys go to the keyboard menu. Press the gun and drag it to the left or the right. Press anywhere in space and drag left or right; the gun barrel will follow your mouse or finger. Let go to fire. The weapon will past through your release point.
